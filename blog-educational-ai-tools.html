Students today can use free AI tools to improve learning, writing, and productivity. Here are the best ones in 2025:

1. Grammarly (Free)

Correct grammar, spelling, and style with AI-powered suggestions.

2. Quillbot

Great for paraphrasing essays and summarizing articles.

3. Notion AI

Organize notes, create study outlines, and brainstorm ideas.

4. Otter.ai

Transcribe lectures and meetings in real-time for free.

5. Google Bard (Free Access)

A study partner for asking questions and generating summaries.

6. Duolingo AI

Learn new languages with AI-based lessons and practice.

Conclusion

These tools help students write better, study smarter, and save time. AI is quickly becoming a must-have for education.
